How is this working?

  1. People experience some kind of network neutrality violation such as blocking, filtering or throttling, they report their findings on our site.
  2. we advertise the new violations to ellicit further confirmations and technical analysis by other customers of the same provider.
  3. we assemble a complete dossier on the violation and submit it to the responsible National Regulatory Authority (NRA)
  4. in 6 months from the start we submit a complete list to Commissioner Kroes.
